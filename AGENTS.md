# AGENTS.md

## Purpose

This repository contains two separate long-term roadmaps:

1. Software Engineering Foundations
2. AI Engineering

Software Engineering Foundations is the current active roadmap. AI Engineering comes later.

## Global Rules

- Preserve existing work.
- Do not delete roadmap material. Archive old or unclear material under `archive/pre-refactor/` or the relevant roadmap archive.
- Keep Software Engineering and AI Engineering separated.
- Do not mix notes, practice, projects, or resources between roadmap folders.
- Do not create web apps, frontend apps, backend apps, Next.js apps, FastAPI apps, or deployment projects unless explicitly asked.
- Keep notes in `notes/`.
- Keep small exercises and drills in `practice/`.
- Keep larger portfolio builds in `projects/`.
- Keep references, reading lists, and cheatsheets in `resources/`.
- Use evidence-based mastery only. Do not mark a topic complete unless its `mastery.md` contains concrete evidence.
- Do not create the full topic scaffold unless explicitly asked.
- Create only active/current folders plus useful roadmap-level documentation.
- Never commit `.env` files or real secrets.

## Current Priority

Work in `roadmaps/software-engineering-foundations/` unless the user explicitly asks for AI roadmap work.

## Roadmap Boundaries

- Software Engineering material belongs in `roadmaps/software-engineering-foundations/`.
- AI Engineering material belongs in `roadmaps/ai-engineering/`.
- Old mixed-layout or unclear material belongs in `archive/pre-refactor/`.
